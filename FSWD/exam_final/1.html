Music is a vital part of different moments of human life. Musical Instruments play a key role in helping to define this artform of music. One such Prominent Musical Instrument is Flute. FLute gained popularity in indian beacause Lord Krishna loved to play this specific instrument.
Flute is a member of a family of musical instruments in the woodwind group. Like all woodwinds, flutes are aerophones, producing sound with a vibrating column of air. Some Types of flute are: Piccolo Flute, Alto Flute, Bass FLute, Wooden Flute, Concert Flute etc. Each type has its own unique specifications but all of them work on the same principle of aerophones and produce melodious sounds for generations to enjoy.
