Christopher Edwin Breaux aka Frank Ocean

Frank Ocean is an American singer, songwriter, and rapper

California, United States of America.

Frank first gained popularity as a member of the hip-hop collective "Odd Future" before releasing his critically acclaimed debut mixtape "Nostalgia, Ultra" in 2011. His album, BLONDE, is a beautifully crafted album that combines raw emotion with intricate production, resulting in a hauntingly beautiful and timeless work of art. Throughout his career, Ocean has been praised for his artistry and has become a cultural icon for his honest and open approach to music and personal life.
